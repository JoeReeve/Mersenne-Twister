More info Every pixel on this canvas is generated using the Mersenne Twister. The algorithm loops through every pixel on the canvas and generates a random RBG value for each one, resulting in a totally random (pseudo-random!) color. The seed used is the current time in milliseconds, so refresh the page to generate a brand new canvas. In theory, because of how many different arrangements of pixels this can generate, the next time you refresh you could see the latest selfie you took looking back at you, or maybe a picture of the surface of Mars! Really it could show you any picture ever taken, or any thought ever imagined, or the answers to your next test!
